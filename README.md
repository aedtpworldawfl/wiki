## 📖 About

**AEDTP WORLD FREE LICENSE (AWFL) WIKI** — also known as **AWFLMETA** — is a community-driven encyclopedia inspired by Wikipedia, but built for continuous publishing, real-time updating, and limitless expansion. Unlike static encyclopedias, AWFLMETA is designed so that articles can grow over time, images can evolve, and knowledge never stops.

The platform was created and is maintained by **AEDTP WORLD**, founded by **Emmanuel Deliver Amable**, with a mission to make free, structured knowledge accessible to everyone across the globe.

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Free & Open** — Anyone can access, browse, and contribute to the wiki without barriers.

- **Transparent Authorship** — Every article displays the publisher name, keywords, and publication date.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Browsing the Wiki

Visit the live wiki at:

**[https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/wiki/](https://aedtpworldawfl.github.io/wiki/)**

- Navigate using the top category menu.
- Use the search bar on any category page to instantly filter articles.
- Articles can be sorted alphabetically or by publication date.

### Publishing an Article

1. **Sign In or Create an Account** on the wiki platform.
2. Navigate to the category where you want to publish.
3. Click **"+ New Wiki"** or **"Create New Wiki Page"**.
4. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Subject / Topic Name** — Up to 100 characters (used as the URL slug).
   - **Keywords** — Up to 50 characters, comma-separated.
   - **Description** — Up to 5,000 characters per submission.
   - **Publisher** — Up to 100 characters (defaults to AEDTP WORLD).
   - **Image** *(optional)* — Square ratio, JPEG or PNG, up to 2MB.
5. Click **"Save & Publish"**.

> 💡 **Tip:** You can publish to the same subject multiple times to update or expand the article over time. Each submission adds to the article's history without exceeding per-submission limits.

---

## 📋 Article Standards

To ensure consistency and usability across AWFLMETA, every article follows these rules:

- **Subject (Topic Name):** Max 100 characters. Serves as the page slug/URL.
- **Keywords:** Max 50 characters, comma-separated. Used for search and filtering.
- **Description:** Max 5,000 characters per submission. Can be expanded via re-publishing.
- **Publisher:** Max 100 characters. Defaults to `AEDTP WORLD` if left blank.
- **Images:** Optional. One image per submission. Must be square ratio, JPEG or PNG, max 2MB.
