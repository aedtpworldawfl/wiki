Buddy DML

Emmanuel Deliver Amable (born 14 April 2008), known professionally as Buddy DML and also by the stage name The Trenchkid TM, is a Ghanaian musical artist, rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. His music is primarily influenced by hip hop, Afrobeat, and melodic trap styles.

 Early life 
Buddy DML was born in Anloga in the Volta Region of Ghana. He developed an early interest in music during childhood, listening to and studying music from an early age. He began singing at the age of thirteen.

 Career 
Buddy DML began recording music in 2021 under the name Emmanuel Deliver DML. He later rebranded professionally as Buddy DML.

Between 2023 and 2024, he became more active in music production and distribution, releasing songs across digital platforms. His music is often described as emotional and reflective, focusing on themes such as struggle, faith, relationships, and personal growth.

 Musical style 
Buddy DML is known for melodic vocal delivery and emotionally driven songwriting. His sound blends Afrobeat rhythms with elements of hip hop, drill, R&B, and trap music.

 Breakthrough 
His breakthrough track is reported to be "Yansh Na Yansh" (2024), which gained attention through social media platforms before being released in full form. The song was later re-released in 2025.
